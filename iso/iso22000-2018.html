What is ISO 22000:2018?

ISO 22000:2018 is an international standard for Food Safety Management Systems (FSMS), focusing on ensuring the safety of food products throughout the entire supply chain, from farm to table. It provides a framework for organizations in the food industry to identify and control food safety hazards, ensuring that food products are safe for consumption. The standard applies to all organizations involved in the food chain, including food producers, processors, distributors, and retailers, regardless of their size or location.

ISO 22000:2018 integrates the principles of Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) and incorporates the Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) cycle to ensure continual improvement in food safety management. It ensures that an organization has processes in place to monitor, assess, and control food safety risks effectively. The certification helps build trust with consumers and regulatory bodies by demonstrating a commitment to maintaining high food safety standards.

Why is ISO 22000:2018 Important?

ISO 22000:2018 is essential for any organization in the food industry because it ensures that food products are safe for consumption by managing food safety hazards. The standard promotes consistent practices to prevent contamination, reduce foodborne illnesses, and meet global food safety regulations. Compliance with ISO 22000:2018 also helps organizations improve their operational efficiency by optimizing processes and ensuring that food safety risks are addressed at every stage of production.

Key Benefits of ISO 22000:2018

  • Enhanced Food Safety: Ensure the safety of food products from contamination.
  • Global Compliance: Meet international food safety regulations.
  • Improved Risk Management: Identify and control food safety hazards effectively.
  • Customer Confidence: Build trust with consumers and regulatory bodies.
  • Market Expansion: Access global markets by demonstrating compliance with international standards.
  • Operational Efficiency: Optimize processes to reduce waste and improve productivity.
